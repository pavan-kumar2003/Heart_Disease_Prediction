Description of Heart Disease Parameters


  • Age: Age of the individual in years. Recommended range: 29-77.

  • Gender: Gender of the individual.
    • 1: Male
    • 0: Female

  • Chest Pain Type:
    • 0: Typical Angina
    • 1: Atypical Angina
    • 2: Non-anginal Pain
    • 3: Asymptomatic

  • Resting Blood Pressure: Resting blood pressure in mm Hg. Recommended range: 94-200.

  • Cholesterol: Serum cholesterol in mg/dl. Recommended range: 126-564.

  • Fasting Blood Sugar: Indicates if fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl.
    • 1: True
    • 0: False

  • Resting Electrocardiographic Results:
    • 0: Normal
    • 1: ST-T wave abnormality
    • 2: Left ventricular hypertrophy

  • Maximum Heart Rate Achieved: Maximum heart rate during exercise. Recommended range: 71-202.

  • Exercise Induced Angina: Indicates if angina is induced by exercise.
    • 1: Yes
    • 0: No

  • Oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest. Recommended range: 0.0-6.2.

  • Slope: Slope of the peak exercise ST segment.
    • 0: Upsloping
    • 1: Flat
    • 2: Downsloping

  • Number of Major Vessels: Number of major vessels (0-3) colored by fluoroscopy. Range: 0-3.

  • Thalassemia:
    • 0: Normal
    • 1: Fixed defect
    • 2: Reversible defect
